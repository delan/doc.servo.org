fn clear(&self, selection: LinuxClipboardKind) -> Result<(), Error>

Performs a “clear” operation on the clipboard, which is implemented by relinquishing the selection to revert its owner to None. This gracefully and comformly informs the X server and any clipboard managers that the data was no longer valid and won’t be offered from our window anymore.

See ask_clipboard_manager_to_request_our_data for more details on why this is important and specification references.
