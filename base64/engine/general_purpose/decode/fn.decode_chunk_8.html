Decode 8 bytes of input into 6 bytes of output.

input is the 8 bytes to decode. index_at_start_of_input is the offset in the overall input (used for reporting errors accurately) decode_table is the lookup table for the particular base64 alphabet. output will have its first 6 bytes overwritten
